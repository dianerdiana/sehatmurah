## Project Overview

SehatMurah is a healthcare booking platform designed to help patients find doctors, book appointments, and manage follow-up interactions in a cleaner digital flow. The project is planned for the capstone window from 01 May 2026 to 10 June 2026 with a focused MVP so the core booking experience can be completed first.

## Planning Assumptions

- The project runs for 41 days total.
- The timeline is split into 4 sprints across the full project window.
- The first release focuses on the core booking and admin workflow.
- Future enhancements are documented separately so they do not delay the MVP.

## Sprint Schedule

| Sprint | Dates | Focus | Deliverables |

## Scope

### In Scope

- User registration, login, and role-based access.
- Patient doctor search and booking workflow.
- Doctor profile and schedule management.
- Admin management for users, doctors, specialists, and reviews.
- Media and profile photo handling already supported by the stack.

### Out of Scope for the MVP

- Telemedicine and live video consultations.
- Payment gateway integration.
- Automated reminders through SMS or WhatsApp.
- AI-driven diagnosis or symptom recommendations.
- Full medical document vault and advanced record-sharing workflow.

## Success Criteria

- The application supports the core appointment journey end to end.
- Admin and doctor workflows are clearly separated by role.
- The documentation reflects what is actually implemented and what is still future work.
- The final deliverable is coherent, clean, and ready for submission.
